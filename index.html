About Us

From its inception in 1998 the Karini - Mukesh Himatsingka Group has evolved as one of the leading automobile dealership groups in eastern India. The group has evolved to a strength of 1600 employees with an annual turnover of 600 crores. Over the last two decades the group has ventured in multiple channels like automobile dealerships, spare part distributorships, fast food restaurants, education & skill development.

Our Services

What we do ?

Automobile Dealership

This Group operates Dealerships of Hyundai Motors India Ltd under the trade name "Mukesh Hyundai" at Kolkata

Education & Skill development

The group ventured into imparting education business in 2008 under the brand of "Mukesh Training Academy"

Hospitality

The group has franchise of restaurant brands like ‘Pizza Hut’ and ‘Oriental Bistro’ in Guwahati along with its own brand called ‘Foodies World’

Sparepart Distributorship

The group has Spare Parts Distributorship of Mobis India Ltd. under the trade name “Mukesh Automotive” in Kolkata
